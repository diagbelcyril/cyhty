ABOUT CYHT LOADOUTS

CYHT Loadouts is a Battle Royale weapon recommendation website for Call of Duty: Mobile players. It helps new and experienced players discover optimized attachments, compare Mythic and Non-Mythic builds, and learn the current weapon meta.

Our goal is to provide accurate, beginner-friendly, and competitive loadouts that improve gameplay performance in every match.
